#### KEY INSIGHT:
1. In January, the two top products were Vanities and Collectibles which generated USD 241,405 and USD 241,075 respectively during this said period.

2. Donald Reynolds lead the Sales Team in terms of revenue acquired from Decoratives in the month of March where he brought in the sum of USD 140,196.

3. Findings from this report shows that there is a strong correlation between Revenue generated per state to the number of stores found in the states.

4. Between the month of January to May, the product category Decoratives had the highest percentage yield of revenue in 30% which is followed by Furniture with 15% yield; the low yielding categories are sports and beddings with 4% each.
